HarvestHub is a crop management system that monitors, controls, and advises on various farm operations in real-time by applying IoT and AI technology. Using data collected from sensors, the program tracks various metrics such as soil type, moisture, pH level, temperature, radiation, nutrients, as well as, external surroundings including rain and wind levels to advise you on what steps you should take next to maximise crop yield. The aim of the program is to make technology-driven agriculture accessible for anybody who desires it.

How it works

  1. Place sensors around the crop field.
  2. Collect data from the sensors with your phone remotely using cellular network or bluetooth via the HarvestHub app.
  3. View information on your soil and other factors which can impact yield in real-time along with visualisations on key metrics informing you on crop health. The program automatically analyses the data collected to personally advise you on any steps that migh help you maximise yield in the long-run.
